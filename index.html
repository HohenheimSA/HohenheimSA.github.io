About us

Company Background

The Edge Funeral Solutions was found by Mr. Zanobuhle Ngcephe in the year 2017. It took over the market as it was then operating as a brokerage company. We have been serving our clients, prioritizing the client’s needs and demands through ensuring that they are fully covered and given dignified products. Miss Sendra Lekala was then appointed as the new managing director and chief executive officer mid 2020 bringing in the vibrancy and zeal implementations of keeping up with time. Our service’s market are diversified across race, religion, age and social belief giving value to everyone who aims for excellence.

Vision

We purport to set one’s heart on incredible solutions by being the funeral insurance home of choice for all families in our community. Known for our compassion, integrity, creativity and values, we set a standard for exceptional quality service by which other funeral insurance businesses will measure themselves.

Mission

We aim to facilitate meaningful ways for families to grieve their loss and celebrate memories of their loved ones exceeding customers expectations and going an extra mile in the services that we provide.

Objectives

  • Adding value to our core services
  • Reduce anxiety and fear
  • Granting security to clients
  • Mobilize savings

Services

Our Services

Covers start from R5 000.00 to R30 000.00 and they are divided into groups tabulating different ages for various covers.

Family Plan

Cover your spouse and your children from as little as R62.00 a month.

1 + 5 Plan

Cover your spouse, children from 18 years of age and extended family members of up to 5 from as little as R90.00 a month.

1 + 9 Plan

Cover your spouse and extended family members of up to 9 from as little as R110.00 a month.

1 + 13 Plan

Cover your spouse, children from 18 years of age and extended family members of up to 13 from as little as R140.00 a month.
